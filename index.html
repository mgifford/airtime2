Airtime2: Meeting Talk-Time Analyzer

Analyze who spoke, for how long, and what they talked about using Zoom or Teams VTT transcripts. No AI, no analytics, just a simple report with a clean transcript of the discussion.

1. Provide transcript

You can upload a .vtt file, drag and drop it, or paste the transcript text directly.


Drag and drop a .vtt file here, or use the upload control.

3. Timeline of speaking

Timeline shows when each speaker talked during the meeting.

4. Word clouds

Shows frequently used words for the main speakers and overall meeting. Filler words and common words are excluded as much as possible. Frequency is indicated by word size.

5. Cleaned transcript

Speaker names are bolded. Click a speaker in any section to lock their highlight, then scroll to see their oral participation.
